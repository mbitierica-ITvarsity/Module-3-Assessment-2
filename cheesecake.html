About

Strawberry Cheesecake is a sweet dessert consisting of one or more layers.

The main, and thickest, layer consists of a mixture of a soft, fresh cheese, eggs, and sugar and strawberries.

There is a bottom layer, it consists of a crust or base made from crushed cookies,graham crackers.
